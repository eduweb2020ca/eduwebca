TEACHING METHODOLOGY

Our primary aim shall always be to develop the understanding of the subject by giving tools to memorize and other means to absorb fully the basics of a subject. Our courses are basically divided into three segments other than being course specific. Level I is basic level understanding is created of the relevant terminology and outline is formed. It is just to form a bird's eye view of the subject and co-relation is established with day to day activities with the subject. At Level II we dive a little deep and form a basic understanding about each topic that needs more understanding. At Level III full fledged discussion on the subject is made. Our teaching methodology is unique as we offer face to face and live online coaching individually for each student ( on request basis) so that the subject teacher and the student are on the same page while deciphering the complex terminologies in simple manner. The main object is to cater to individual needs of the student which may not be addressed in a class room environment.

TEACHER CONNECTIVITY

Students often face difficulties and doubts while studying at home and considering this fact we provide connectivity to the students so that they can solve their problems at the same time. Students can easily clear their doubts through emails anywhere, anytime. However, if it requires personal interaction, a request for doubt class will be accommodated at priority.

Note: We do not make any claims as to 24*7 connectivity or multiple modes of communication to reach out to teachers. The idea is to simply make the students focus during classes, being specific of their doubts makes them think more by themselves and thus develops their understanding of the subject. Remember, teacher can only assist in understanding and we by no means are creating parrots.
